About us

ASHRANDA name is inspired from the local Nubian language in southern Egypt, which is an unwritten language spoken by the people of the Nubia region in southern Egypt. The name ASHRANDA means in the Nubian language “the most beautiful girl in the world.” Logo is inspired from the shape of the anchor which used in the cruise ships and refers too to the sail and it gives too the shape of letter A which is the first letter of the name and repeated three times in the beginning, middle and end of the name. The color of the logo is Gold which is inspired of Nubia too. Nubia is known in the ancient Egyptian time as “The Land of Gold”.
